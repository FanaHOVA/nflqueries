About NFL Queries

NFL Queries lets you search for NFL facts through natural language rather than boring dropdown menus.

At the moment you can search for weight, jersey number, college and number of years in the NFL. Players can be filtered by team and there's a 'injured' option so that you can easily lookup injured players on each team. The database has been created with the Python library nflgame and that's what I'm gonna use to keep it updated.
